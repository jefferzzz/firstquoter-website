Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This policy explains what personal data FirstQuoter collects when you use the app and when your customers interact with a quote acceptance page. We collect only what we need to provide the service, we don't sell your data, and we aim to be straightforward about how everything works. If you have questions, email us at hello@firstquoter.com.

1. Who we are

FirstQuoter is a trading name of Jeffrey Nathan Green, operating as a sole trader in the United Kingdom. References to "FirstQuoter", "we", "us" or "our" throughout this policy refer to Jeffrey Nathan Green trading as FirstQuoter.

For the purposes of UK data protection law, we are the data controller for personal data collected from app users. For personal data belonging to your customers that you enter into the app, you are the data controller and FirstQuoter acts as a data processor on your behalf.

You can contact us at hello@firstquoter.com.

2. Data we collect from app users

When you create an account and use the FirstQuoter app, we may collect:

  • Account information — your name, email address, and business name when you register.
  • Usage data — information about how you use the app, such as features accessed and documents created, used to improve the service.
  • Device information — device type, operating system version, and app version, used for troubleshooting and compatibility.
  • Subscription data — whether you are on the Free or Pro plan. Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple or Google; we do not receive or store your payment card details.

3. Data we collect from your customers

When you create a quote or invoice in the app and share it with a customer, the following data is processed:

  • Customer contact details — name, email address, and any address information you enter when creating the document.
  • Acceptance and signature data — if your customer accepts a quote via the online acceptance page (hosted at firstquoter.app), we record the timestamp of acceptance and any digital signature they provide.

You are responsible for ensuring you have a lawful basis to share your customers' personal data with FirstQuoter. See Section 6 for more detail.

4. How we use your data

We use the data we collect to:

  • Provide, operate and maintain the FirstQuoter service.
  • Generate, send and store your quotes and invoices.
  • Enable the quote acceptance and digital signature workflow.
  • Send transactional emails (e.g. quote acceptance notifications to you).
  • Manage your account and subscription.
  • Diagnose technical problems and improve the reliability of the service.
  • Comply with our legal obligations.

We do not use your data for advertising purposes, and we do not sell or rent your data to third parties.

5. Legal basis for processing

Under UK GDPR, we process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Contract performance — processing your account data and documents is necessary to provide the service you have signed up for.
  • Legitimate interests — we may process usage and device data to maintain and improve the service, where this does not override your rights.
  • Legal obligation — we may retain certain records where required by law.

6. Your customers' data and your responsibilities

When you use FirstQuoter to send quotes and invoices, you are providing us with personal data about your customers (such as names and email addresses). In this context:

  • You are the data controller — you decide what data to enter and have a relationship with your customers.
  • FirstQuoter acts as a data processor — we process this data solely to provide the service to you.
  • You are responsible for having a lawful basis to process your customers' personal data and for directing it to FirstQuoter's systems.
  • You should ensure your customers are aware that their details may be processed by a third-party service provider for the purpose of receiving quotes and invoices from you.

7. Data sharing

We do not sell, trade or rent your personal data to third parties. We may share data in limited circumstances:

  • Service providers — we use reputable third-party services to host and operate FirstQuoter (such as cloud infrastructure and email delivery providers). These providers act as processors on our behalf and are bound by data processing agreements.
  • Legal requirements — we may disclose data if required to do so by law, court order, or regulatory authority.
  • Business transfers — if FirstQuoter is acquired or its assets transferred, user data may form part of that transfer. We will notify affected users in advance where possible.

8. Data retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are legally required to retain records for longer (for example, financial records under UK tax law).

Quote and invoice records associated with your account are retained for as long as your account exists. After account deletion, these are removed alongside your account data within the 30-day window described above.

9. Cookies and tracking

The FirstQuoter mobile app does not use cookies. The customer quote acceptance page (hosted at firstquoter.app) does not use tracking cookies or analytics. No tracking pixels or open-tracking mechanisms are used in quote or invoice emails.

10. Your rights under UK GDPR

You have the following rights in relation to your personal data:

  • Right of access — you can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification — you can ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure — you can ask us to delete your data, subject to any legal retention obligations.
  • Right to restriction — you can ask us to limit how we process your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to data portability — you can request your data in a portable format where technically feasible.
  • Right to object — you can object to processing based on legitimate interests.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@firstquoter.com. We will respond within one month. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

11. Security

We take the security of your data seriously. We use industry-standard measures including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS) and secure cloud infrastructure. Access to production systems is restricted to authorised personnel only.

No system is completely secure. If you become aware of any security concern, please contact us immediately at hello@firstquoter.com.

12. International data transfers

We aim to store and process your data within the UK or the European Economic Area wherever possible. Where we use third-party providers that process data outside these regions, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place in line with UK GDPR requirements (such as the UK International Data Transfer Agreement or adequacy decisions).

13. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by email or via an in-app notification before the changes take effect. The date at the top of this page reflects when it was last updated.

Questions about this policy?

Email us at hello@firstquoter.com and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.

For formal data protection enquiries: legal@firstquoter.com
